About us

ANGEL CITY WOOD SHOP


Our Shop At Angel City Woodshop we are committed to high quality woodworking and custom projects that celebrate quality, craftsmanship, and beauty. We offer services for residential, commercial, and industry projects. Our shop is located just south of downtown Los Angeles and features a showroom and fully-equipped woodshop. Paul Moorhead and a small team of woodworkers operate the shop Monday through Friday from 8:00am-4:30pm. Our shop is currently open to the public for tours and information. If you'd like to speak with us about a project, please don't hesitate to contact us or visit our shop. We look forward to serving you and creating something spectacular together.

CNC SERVICES

Our shop provides CNC services for highly-precise and complex cuts. We use this machining process for prototyping and production and extend the service to our clients. CNC stands for "computer numerical control" and it describes the computerized control of subtractive manufacturing. The CNC router is incredibly useful for complex geometry, sculptural designs and perfectly square panels.

DESIGN AND 3D-MODELING

In need of design direction? Our team can help you through the design process- from concept to production. We believe in strong communication and collaboration with our clients. If you already have a vision and are in need of a 3d model, we can help model your design in Autodesk Fusion 360. This model can be used for a variety of purposes and is hosted on the Autodesk cloud service for easy file-sharing.
